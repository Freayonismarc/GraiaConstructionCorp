About us

GRAIA Construction Corporation taces its humble beginnings on November 4, 2003 when then, GRAIA Construction and supply; a sole proprietorship enterprise; began supplying construction materials for various projects in Cavite, Metro Manila, Bataan and Palawan.

After Almost three years of patience, dedicated work and determination to learn the construction business, the enterprise was abe to etch it's place in the local construction industry . GRAIA Construction Corporation was able to secure projects to as far as Rizal, Batangas, Quezon and Leyte.
