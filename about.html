History

GRAIA Construction Corporation traces its humble beginnings on November 4, 2003 when then, GRAIA Construction and supply; a sole proprietorship enterprise; began supplying construction materials for various projects in Cavite, Metro Manila, Bataan and Palawan.

After Almost three years of patience, dedicated work and determination to learn the construction business, the enterprise was abe to etch it's place in the local construction industry . GRAIA Construction Corporation was able to secure projects to as far as Rizal, Batangas, Quezon and Leyte.

Since then, the company evolved into a full pledged construction corporation doing mostly repair and maintenance projects from DPWH and other Local Government Units. The company was able to acquire various equipment and machinery to support its operations. At present, GRAIA Construction Corporation is widely patronized for its quality workmanship and commitment to deliver the products and services and finish the projects on time.
